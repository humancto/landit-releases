Features

Everything you need to land the job

Eight powerful modules working together. From finding the right opportunity to acing the final interview.

AI Job Discovery

Search 7+ sources simultaneously: Indeed, LinkedIn, Greenhouse, Lever, RemoteOK, HN Who's Hiring, Adzuna, and more. AI scores every job 0-100 against your resume.

Live Mock Interviews

Voice-powered practice with Gemini Live API. Technical, behavioral, and system design interviews with real-time AI feedback and performance analytics.

Pipeline Tracking

Kanban board to manage applications from discovery to offer. Track status, follow-up dates, interview rounds, and compare multiple offers side by side.

Career Coach

AI-powered career guidance that analyzes your profile, identifies skill gaps, and provides actionable improvement plans tailored to your target roles.

Resume & Cover Letter

AI analysis of your resume with targeted suggestions. Auto-generated cover letters customized for each position. Skill gap breakdowns for every job.

Market Intelligence

Salary benchmarks, market trends, and competitive analysis. Understand your worth with data-driven insights powered by real market data.

Smart Follow-ups

Automatic ghost detection when applications go silent. Timely follow-up reminders so no opportunity falls through the cracks.

Answer Vault

Save and reuse answers to common application questions. Auto-fill responses across applications to save hours of repetitive typing.

How It Works

Three steps to your next role

Set up once, then let Landit do the heavy lifting.

1

Configure

Upload your resume, set target roles and locations, and add your AI provider API keys. Landit supports Gemini, OpenAI, Anthropic, and Groq.

2

Discover

Landit searches 7+ job sources simultaneously, deduplicates results, and scores every job against your profile. The best matches rise to the top.

3

Land It

Track applications on a Kanban board, practice with AI mock interviews, generate cover letters, and get timely follow-up reminders. Close the deal.

Privacy First

Your data never leaves your Mac

Landit is built local-first. No accounts, no cloud, no tracking. Your career data stays where it belongs.

Local SQLite Database

All your data lives in a SQLite database on your Mac. No servers, no cloud storage, no external dependencies.

No Account Required

Download, open, and start using. No sign-up, no email, no friction. Your career search is nobody's business.

Opt-in Analytics Only

Analytics is completely optional and off by default. When enabled, it only sends anonymous usage patterns. Never your personal data.
